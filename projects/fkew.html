Florida Kids Eat Well

Branding and responsive web design for a healthy eating organization in Florida

Branding / Web Design

Florida Kids Eat Well is an organization to promote healthy eating habits. They wanted their brand identity to be fun, inviting, and current. Their logo needed to be flexible and reflect their brand and core values. I created a speech bubble with the inside of an orange to reflect the location, the conversational nature of the site, and their promotion of healthy eating.

A challenge of this website was to create a space that satisfied a variety of audiences - kids, parents, nutritionists, and teachers. To solve for this, I designed a splash page so that when users arrive, they can choose which section to explore so that they find the most relevant information quickly.
