Kate's Cakes

Branding and web design for a cupcake business

Branding / Web Design

For the branding of this company, I wanted the look and feel to be light, simple and clean. I chose feminine elements and mixed them with modern touches to be user friendly and inviting. The web design reflects the brand by having grouped content that is easy to follow and a fixed side navigation for easy scrolling. I also wanted to focus on the imagery, so I kept the content and colors minimal.
