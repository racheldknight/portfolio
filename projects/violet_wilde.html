Violet & Wilde

Logo for a fine art photography gallery

Logo Design

I designed a logo to match the company's brand - elegant, minimalistic and clean. Given the design of their site, I created a logo that is visually interesting, but could be overlaid on images or color and not be obtrusive or distracting. Their focus on details and getting to the point inspired my design to be focused on a single color and strong, dynamic lettering.
