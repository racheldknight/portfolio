RV Egg

Branding and web design for a breakfast sandwhich food truck in Richmond, VA

Branding / Web Design

RV Egg is a play on "RVA" for Richmond, VA. I wanted the food and the story to inspire the web design and brand. The look and feel reflects a vintage and retro theme that would appeal to the key audience.

Given that the truck is mobile and most people would be looking for the truck "on the go", I made the web design very mobile first. I kept the content and the layout minimal so that users can quickly find the information they need in the moment.
