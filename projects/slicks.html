Slick's Auto Shop

Web design and front-end development for an auto shop

Web Design / Front-end Development / Wordpress

My design for this project was based on giving the user the most important information upfront. I did not use many colors and instead focused on the structure and content. I made contact information readily available, so once they find the information they need, they can easily contact Slick's. A simple layout keeps the information succient and simple.

I used Skeleton as a framework to develop I utilized a responsive, custom Wordpress template design for this site. It was important for the client to be able to update items on their own, so I utilized plugins and widgets. With custom page templates, I created a better CMS experience. I also used HTML, CSS, and PHP to complete this project.
